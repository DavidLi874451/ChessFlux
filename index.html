Welcome to ChessFlux!

We are so glad for you to be able to join this amazing community of like-minded individuals and cannot wait to assist you with your chess endeavors!

ChessFlux is a community designed to assist all aspiring chess players towards becoming stronger players. This club provides several opportunities for individuals from all chess backgrounds to improve their game as they receive free coaching and guidance from professional coaches and top players.

We frequently post instructional articles and lessons that help individuals expand their sphere of chess knowledge for free. We also provide multiple chess exercises for all individuals to attempt and learn from.
